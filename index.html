Who I am and how I think

I specialize in being the customer’s voice in the room while also speaking the language of engineering, product, and finance. My background spans product management, technical program management, and SaaS delivery across both Fortune 100 enterprises and high-growth startups.

In practice, that means I can sit with your customer, sales, or business stakeholders to unpack the real problem, then turn that into structured requirements, user stories, and product decisions that your technology teams can execute on with confidence.

Experience & impact

What it feels like to work together

While every engagement is different, clients typically come to me when they say things like: “We know what we want in our heads, but we can’t seem to get it clarified and built.”

  • You stop losing time in translation. Meetings shift from “what are we even building?” to clear decisions, priorities, and next steps.
  • Customers feel genuinely heard. Their problems are accurately captured and reflected in the way your product evolves.
  • Engineering gets actionable requirements. No more vague requests—just grounded user stories with context, constraints, and clear acceptance criteria.
  • Leadership sees the link from vision → roadmap → delivery. You gain a narrative that connects strategy, execution, and measurable outcomes.
From fuzzy ask → concrete scope From “fire drills” → predictable delivery From assumptions → customer-backed decisions

Example engagement formats:

  • Intensive 4–6 week discovery to clarify a new initiative or product line.
  • Fractional product / TPM for 1–3 days per week to drive a strategic project.
  • Advisory partner for founders or leaders who need an experienced sounding board at key decision points.

We’ll right-size the engagement to your stage, budget, and urgency.
